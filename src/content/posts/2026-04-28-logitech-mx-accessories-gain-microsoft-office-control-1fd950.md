## Logitech Brings Office Control to MX Accessories

Logitech has announced that its Productivity Plugins now support Microsoft Office apps, including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, on its entire MX line of accessories, including the MX Creative Console. This development allows users to control these Office applications with custom shortcuts, enhancing their productivity. The MX Creative Console, which launched in September 2024, has been expanding its capabilities with plug-ins that support creativity-focused apps such as Final Cut Pro, Adobe Lightroom, and Figma.

## How It Works

The MX accessories use Logitech's Actions Ring menus, which provide a customizable interface for accessing frequently used actions. Users can access shortcuts, actions, and specific tools in Office apps. For instance, with the MX Creative Console, users can now directly interact with Microsoft Office applications, streamlining their workflow. The integration is facilitated through Logitech's proprietary software, enabling users to customize their experience by creating shortcuts and actions specific to their needs.
