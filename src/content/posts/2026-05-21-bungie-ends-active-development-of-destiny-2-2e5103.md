Bungie is moving on from Destiny 2, announcing that the game's active development will end on June 9. This decision marks a significant shift in the studio's focus towards a new beginning. The final update, set to release on June 9, will include various changes aimed at making Destiny 2 a welcoming place for players to return to. The update will bring several enhancements and adjustments to the game, ensuring that players have a positive experience as they continue to play.

The announcement post provides detailed information about the final update, with more details to be shared up to and shortly after its launch. After that, Bungie's weekly blog entries will enter a new phase. Although the game's active development is ending, Destiny 2 will remain playable, much like the first Destiny. Bungie has assured that the game will continue to receive support, albeit in a different form.
