## Nvidia's ChatRTX Update
Nvidia's ChatRTX app has received an update, adding new features such as photo search capabilities, AI-powered speech recognition, and compatibility with more LLMs. The app represents Nvidia's demonstration of the power of AI when fueled by consumer RTX graphics cards. ChatRTX offers a ChatGPT-adjacent experience locally installed on the user's computer, allowing them to leverage their data into AI use cases without the risk of having that data stolen. This update is significant, as it showcases the potential of AI in various applications.

## Vendors Dislike Draft Federal Acquisition Regulation Update
Vendors are unhappy with a draft update to the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) that would require them to hand over keys to their networks and report intrusions within eight hours of discovery. The proposed rules, developed by the Department of Defense, General Services Administration, and NASA, aim to improve government security. However, industry respondents argue that the rules are burdensome and inconsistent with other reporting rules. This development highlights the ongoing tension between government agencies and tech companies regarding data security and access.
