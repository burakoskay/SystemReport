Uber's new doorstep return pickup service allows users to schedule a courier to collect returns from their homes for a fee. The service aims to simplify the return process. There are limitations to the service, which are outlined within the Uber app.

This move is part of Uber's strategy to expand beyond ride-hailing. The company is actively adding services to become a one-stop-shop for daily needs. Doorstep return pickup caters to growing demand for convenient logistics.

The courier fee and service limitations are key factors. If executed well, this could impact the delivery and logistics market.
