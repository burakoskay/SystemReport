## Musk's Testimony at OpenAI Trial

Elon Musk took the stand at his OpenAI trial, recounting a story he's told before - in interviews and to author Walter Isaacson for his biography. This was the first time he shared it under oath.

Musk's testimony is a significant development in the ongoing trial. The trial itself is a result of Musk's complicated history with OpenAI, an organization he co-founded.

## Background on OpenAI and Musk's Involvement

OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Musk, Sam Altman, and others. The organization's mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Musk has been a vocal advocate for AI safety and has expressed concerns about the potential risks of advanced AI systems.

Musk's relationship with OpenAI has been complicated. He left the organization's board in 2018, citing concerns about the company's direction. Since then, he has been critical of OpenAI's approach to AI development.
