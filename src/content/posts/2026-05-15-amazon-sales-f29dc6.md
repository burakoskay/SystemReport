## History of Prime Day

Prime Day was first launched in 2015 as a way to celebrate Amazon's 20th anniversary. The event was initially met with skepticism, but it quickly gained popularity and has since become one of the biggest shopping events of the year. Today, Prime Day is a global event, with millions of shoppers participating in the sale.

Over the years, Prime Day has evolved to include more features and benefits for shoppers. For example, Amazon has introduced new deals and discounts, as well as enhanced shopping experiences, such as live streaming and augmented reality. The event has also become a major platform for small businesses and emerging brands to reach a wider audience.
