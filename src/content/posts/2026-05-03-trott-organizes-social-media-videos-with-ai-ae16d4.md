## Trott Takes on Social Media Video Chaos

Scrolling through hundreds of social media posts to find that one recipe, product, or tutorial video is a frustrating reality for many users. Trott, a new app, aims to change that by using AI to organize and extract key details from videos on platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok.

The app allows users to share reels, shorts, and videos directly to Trott, where AI-powered organization and smart integrations come into play. For instance, users can extract locations, recipes, steps, or product details based on the content type using Large Language Models (LLMs). The app also features fuzzy search, AI chat, and smart integrations like maps for travel, recipe cards for cooking, and tutorials for learning.

## How Trott Works

Trott's capabilities were showcased in a demo on Hacker News, where the app's creator highlighted its ability to categorize and group content by topic, genre, or collection. The app's AI chat function allows users to ask questions like 'show me those aesthetic cafes in Bali' and receive relevant results from their saved reels. 

One beta tester described the app as 'insane,' saying they had over 1000 travel reels saved but never looked at them until now. With Trott, they were able to plan their trip to Goa using the organized content. Another tester was impressed by the app's chat function, saying it 'blew their mind' when it found every single reel they had saved months ago.

## The Problem Trott Solves

Manual organization of social media content is a significant pain point for users. Current methods involve tedious scrolling and often result in lost or forgotten content. Trott's AI-powered solution aims to alleviate this issue by providing a more efficient way to search, sort, and extract details from social media videos.
