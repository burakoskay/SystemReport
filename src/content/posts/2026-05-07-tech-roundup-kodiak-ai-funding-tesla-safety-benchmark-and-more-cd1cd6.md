## Tesla Model Y Meets Safety Benchmark

The Tesla Model Y is the first car to meet the new US driver assistance safety benchmark. The benchmark rating applies to 2026 Tesla Model Y vehicles assembled on or after November 12, 2025. ## Loopletter: Open-Source Email Marketing

Loopletter is an open-source email marketing platform for independent artists and creators. Built with Next.js, Supabase, and AWS SES, Loopletter helps artists own their audience, automate campaigns, and understand performance. The platform's source code is available on GitHub, and contributors are welcome to help develop the project. Loopletter's focus on open-source and transparency sets it apart from proprietary email marketing platforms.

The open-source nature of Loopletter is a significant advantage for independent artists and creators. The platform's transparency and flexibility allow users to customize and extend the platform to meet their needs. The community-driven approach to development ensures that the platform will continue to evolve and improve over time.
