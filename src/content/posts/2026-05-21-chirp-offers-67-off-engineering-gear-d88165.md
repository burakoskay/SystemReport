These numbers matter because many engineers spend a significant portion of their paycheck on hardware and peripherals. A 67% cut on a set of wheels can turn a $300 purchase into a $100 spend. For instance, a developer eyeing a high-end graphics card for machine learning projects could allocate that saved amount towards a more advanced model or other professional development opportunities.

The savings from these discounts can have a direct impact on an engineer's ability to invest in their career. By allocating resources that would have been spent on gear towards professional development, engineers can improve their skills and become more competitive in the job market.
