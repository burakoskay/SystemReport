# eBay Rejects GameStop's $56B Takeover Bid

GameStop's $56 billion takeover offer for eBay was rejected by eBay's board, which called it "neither credible nor attractive."

## The Bid and the Rejection

GameStop's offer was met with skepticism by eBay's board, which questioned the video game retailer's financials and its ability to integrate eBay's operations. eBay's board of directors has a fiduciary duty to consider all proposals, but in this case, they concluded that GameStop's bid was not in the best interest of the company or its shareholders.

## The Stakes

The rejection deals a setback to GameStop's expansion plans. A successful acquisition of eBay would have given GameStop a major presence in the e-commerce space. eBay's market value is around $27 billion, according to recent reports.
