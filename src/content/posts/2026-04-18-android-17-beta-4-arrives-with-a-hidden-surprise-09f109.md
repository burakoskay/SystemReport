Google has just dropped Android 17 Beta 4, the final beta update before the full release. The Easter egg, which hadn't been updated in three generations, is now live and features a connect-the-dots puzzle. Solving the puzzle kicks off a new animation, adding a touch of whimsy to the Android experience.

Android 17 Beta 4 is now available for Pixel devices, marking the end of the preview cycle. While this is the last scheduled beta release, Google may still roll out patches in the coming weeks to iron out any remaining issues.

The Easter egg showcases Google's attention to detail and commitment to delivering a unique user experience. Users can explore the new features and Easter eggs that Google has in store with the full release of Android 17.
