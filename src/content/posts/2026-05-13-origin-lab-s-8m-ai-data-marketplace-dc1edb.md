## Origin Lab Secures $8M for AI Data Sales

Origin Lab, backed by $8M in funding, will create a marketplace for video game companies to sell data to AI developers building world models. The company will serve as an intermediary, allowing game firms to monetize their existing data assets. Investors in the funding round are not disclosed.

The funding round indicates a growing demand for high-quality data to train AI models. World models, a type of AI, require vast amounts of data to learn and improve. By providing a platform for game companies to sell their data, Origin Lab aims to capitalize on this demand.
