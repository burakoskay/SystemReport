## Peec's Rapid Growth
Peec, one of Berlin's rising startups, has more than doubled its annualized revenue in months to $10M, according to sources. This rapid growth is a testament to the company's innovative approach to helping brands track their presence in AI searches.

Peec's success can be attributed to its ability to provide valuable insights to brands, allowing them to better understand their online presence and make data-driven decisions. The company's technology is designed to help brands navigate the complex world of AI searches, where traditional search engine optimization (SEO) strategies may not be effective.
