## FCC Anti-Discrimination Rule Struck Down

Chairman Brendan Carr celebrated the FCC court loss, as reported by Ars Technica. The ruling may have significant implications for internet providers and their policies.

The FCC's anti-discrimination rule aimed to prevent internet providers from discriminating against certain groups of users. The rule's opponents argued that it would impose unnecessary regulations on internet providers. The court's decision is a victory for internet providers, who can now operate with more freedom.
