## Defense Startup Firestorm Labs Raises $82M

Firestorm Labs, a defense technology startup, has secured $82 million in funding to develop deployable drone factories. These factories, designed to fit inside shipping containers, aim to bring manufacturing capabilities to the front lines. The deployable drone factories are designed to be transportable and can be set up quickly in various environments. This capability could significantly enhance the logistics and operational flexibility of defense operations.
