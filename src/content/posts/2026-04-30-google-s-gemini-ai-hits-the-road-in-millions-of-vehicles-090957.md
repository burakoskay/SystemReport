## Google's Gemini AI Assistant Rolls Out to Millions of Vehicles

Google's Gemini AI assistant is hitting the road in millions of vehicles, marking a significant step in the company's push to bring more advanced, conversational AI into the driving experience. This rollout follows the launch of Gemini on Android Auto and its replacement of the Google Assistant on vehicles with Android Automotive. The Gemini app is now available on Android and iOS, with features like notebooks and Liquid Glass. For Android Automotive, Gemini is replacing the Google Assistant, offering a more conversational and integrated experience.

The integration of AI in vehicles is part of a broader trend of tech companies enhancing in-car experiences. With the rise of electric vehicles and autonomous driving technologies, the role of AI in vehicles is becoming increasingly critical. Google's Gemini AI assistant is poised to play a significant role in this evolution, offering drivers a more intuitive and interactive experience. Specifically, Gemini's features such as notebooks and Liquid Glass on Android and iOS respectively, are designed to provide users with a seamless experience across different platforms.
