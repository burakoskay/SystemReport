## Convective Capital Shifts Focus to Disaster Resilience

Convective Capital, a venture capital firm initially focused on fire technology investments, has raised an $85 million fund to broaden its investments to disaster resilience. This new fund marks a significant pivot for the firm, indicating a strategic shift in its investment approach.

## Investment Strategy and Portfolio

The firm's new fund will target investments in disaster resilience, specifically focusing on supporting companies at the seed and early stages of development. Convective Capital aims to provide crucial funding to entrepreneurs and startups working on innovative solutions to mitigate the impact of disasters.
