## CopilotKit Secures $27M to Streamline AI Agent Deployment

CopilotKit, a Seattle-based startup, has raised $27 million in a Series A funding round led by Glilot Capital, NFX, and SignalFire. The company aims to help developers deploy app-native AI agents more efficiently. This significant investment demonstrates investor confidence in CopilotKit's vision and technology.

## What CopilotKit Does

CopilotKit's platform allows developers to integrate AI agents into their applications, enhancing user experience and automating tasks. The startup's solution focuses on making it easier for developers to deploy and manage these AI agents. By streamlining the process, CopilotKit enables developers to focus on building core features rather than getting bogged down in the complexities of AI integration.
