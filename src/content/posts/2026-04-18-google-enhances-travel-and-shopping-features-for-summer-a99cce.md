Google is rolling out updates to enhance travel planning and shopping. A new hotel price tracking feature allows users to monitor prices for specific hotels, helping them make informed booking decisions. Additionally, the company's AI Mode, which helps find in-stock products nearby, is now available. This feature is particularly useful for finding local items like sunscreen or beach gear.

The Gemini app is also getting an update. Notebooks, previously exclusive to Google AI subscribers, are now free for all users. This expansion brings more functionality to the app.

The hotel price-tracking tool is designed to help users save money on summer vacations. With these updates, Google aims to provide users with more detailed information and tools to plan their trips and find what they need locally.
