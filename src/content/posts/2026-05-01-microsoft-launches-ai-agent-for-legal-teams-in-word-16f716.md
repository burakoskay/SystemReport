## Microsoft's Foray into Legal AI

Microsoft has launched a new AI agent in Word, specifically designed for legal teams. This tool, called Legal Agent, aims to assist with tasks such as reviewing contracts, handling document edits, and managing complex documents. The Legal Agent follows structured workflows shaped by real legal practice, managing clearly defined, repeatable tasks like reviewing contracts clause by clause against a playbook. According to Sumit Chauhan, corporate vice president of Microsoft's Office Product Group, this approach allows the agent to work effectively with existing documents that have tracked changes.

## How Legal Agent Works

The agent is designed to work with existing documents, analyzing and making edits as needed. It can handle complex documents and provide a detailed review of contracts. Microsoft's approach with Legal Agent is to provide a more specialized AI tool, one that is focused on the specific needs of legal teams. This could potentially make the tool more effective for these users. For instance, Legal Agent can help lawyers to quickly review and negotiate contracts, reducing the time and effort required for these tasks.
