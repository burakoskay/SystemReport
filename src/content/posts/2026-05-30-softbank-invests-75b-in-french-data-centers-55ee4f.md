## Introduction to SoftBank's Investment
SoftBank will invest up to €75 billion to build French data centers, aiming for 5 GW capacity. This investment marks a significant move by the company to expand its presence in the European data center market.
## Background on Investment
The goal is to develop and operate up to 5 gigawatts of additional data center capacity, according to SoftBank. This expansion is expected to support the growing demand for cloud computing and data storage in the region.

SoftBank's announcement did not provide a specific timeline or details on the financing structure, partners, or investors involved in the project. However, the company's decision to invest in French data centers reflects its confidence in the country's favorable business environment and access to skilled workforce.
