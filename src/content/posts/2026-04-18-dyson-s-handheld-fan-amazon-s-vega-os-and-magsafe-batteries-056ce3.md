## Dyson's Handheld Fan

Dyson's $99.99 HushJet Mini Cool is a slim and sleek handheld fan. The fan is more powerful and louder than expected. Its design is a key selling point, making it a great option for those looking for a portable fan.

At an outdoor wedding, this fan would have been useful. However, its loud noise might have been a distraction.

## Amazon's Vega OS

Amazon launched its newest Fire TV Stick with Vega OS, and all future devices will use Vega OS instead of Android. This change allows Amazon to push updates without Google's licensing constraints.

Amazon's move to Vega OS may reshape the competitive landscape. Competitors that still use Android, such as Roku and Chromecast, retain a broader developer base.

## MagSafe Batteries

MagSafe battery makers prioritize thinness over capacity. Ultra-slim MagSafe packs are designed to be pocketable without distorting a phone's silhouette.

The market's demand for genuine battery endurance keeps larger packs viable for power-hungry users. Manufacturers should explore hybrid designs that retain thinness while increasing capacity.

## Consumer Hardware

The trade-offs between performance, silence, ecosystem openness, and form factor will shape the next wave of consumer hardware. Manufacturers must find a balance between these factors to succeed. Dyson could improve its fan's design, Amazon must supply robust developer tools, and MagSafe producers should explore hybrid designs.
