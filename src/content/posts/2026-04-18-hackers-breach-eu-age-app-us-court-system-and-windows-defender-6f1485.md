## EU Age-Verification App Compromised
Researchers demonstrated a proof-of-concept attack on the European Union's age-verification app in under two minutes. The breach was publicly disclosed.

## Supreme Court Filing System Breach Leads to Probation
Nicholas Moore infiltrated three U.S. government networks, including the Supreme Court's electronic filing system, using stolen credentials. A federal court sentenced Moore to probation.

## Windows Defender Flaws Turned Into Real Attacks
A security researcher disclosed three zero-day vulnerabilities in Windows Defender. Hackers then exploited those flaws in the wild, bypassing endpoint protection.

## Additional Incidents
Major data breaches occurred at a gym chain and a hotel giant. A distributed denial-of-service attack caused disruptions to Bluesky. There are concerns about dubious ICE hires.

## What the Pattern Reveals
The incidents share a common thread: a reliance on untested or poorly configured security controls. Policymakers, government agencies, software vendors, and service providers must take action to harden security.
