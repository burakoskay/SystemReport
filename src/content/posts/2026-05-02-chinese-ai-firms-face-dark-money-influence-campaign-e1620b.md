## Build American AI's Influence Campaign

Build American AI, a nonprofit tied to a super PAC backed by OpenAI and Andreessen Horowitz executives, is funding a campaign to spread pro-AI messaging and stoke fears about China. The group is paying influencers to frame Chinese AI as a threat. This campaign's goal is to promote American AI and gain support for stricter regulations on Chinese AI companies. However, some experts worry that this campaign could lead to increased tensions between the US and China.

The campaign is part of a broader effort to shape public opinion and policy debates around AI. By promoting American AI and stoking fears about Chinese AI, Build American AI aims to create a favorable environment for US-based AI companies. However, critics argue that this campaign could have unintended consequences, such as escalating tensions between the US and China.
