## OpenAI Limits GPT-5.5 Cyber Access
OpenAI will roll out its cybersecurity testing tool, GPT-5.5 Cyber, to critical cyber defenders only. The company announced the limited access in a brief statement.

The restriction limits the pool of users who can probe the model's defensive strengths. OpenAI has not disclosed the criteria for critical cyber defenders, leaving the definition vague.

## Yubico Partnership Adds Hardware Protection
OpenAI launched an opt-in security layer for ChatGPT accounts that uses hardware security keys from Yubico. Users who enable the feature must insert a Yubico key or use its NFC capability to complete authentication.

## Mac Apps Update After Security Issue
OpenAI asked macOS users to update its desktop apps, ChatGPT and Codex, due to a security issue with a third-party developer tool, Axios. The update addresses the security issue and refreshes the security certifications that verify the apps as legitimate OpenAI software.
