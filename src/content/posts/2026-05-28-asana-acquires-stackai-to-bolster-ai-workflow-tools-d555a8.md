## Asana Expands AI Workflow Tools with StackAI Acquisition

Asana has acquired StackAI, a no-code agent-builder, to enhance its automation capabilities. This move is part of Asana's strategy to help businesses automate workflows and improve productivity. The acquisition brings StackAI's no-code agent-builder into Asana's growing suite of AI workflow tools, allowing users to create tailored automation solutions.

## What StackAI Brings to Asana

StackAI's technology enables users to build custom AI agents without requiring extensive coding knowledge. Asana will incorporate this technology into its platform, providing users with more flexibility and customization options. The acquisition supports Asana's efforts to help businesses automate workflows and improve productivity.
