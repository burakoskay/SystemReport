## Cannabis Kiosks Aim to Simplify Access for Seniors

Two cannabis-based companies, LoveBud and Life Is Chill, are introducing user-friendly kiosks to simplify access to cannabis products for seniors. The kiosks are designed to address common barriers older adults face with technology, such as difficulty navigating online platforms or using smartphones.

The kiosks allow users to browse products by category, including options tailored to seniors such as edibles and topicals. A help feature connects users directly to a live representative for assistance or medical-related questions. This initiative aims to provide a more accessible and user-friendly experience for seniors, who are increasingly interested in using cannabis products.

The development of cannabis kiosks reflects a broader trend of companies adapting to the needs of older adults. As the population ages, companies are recognizing the importance of providing accessible and user-friendly products and services.
