## Apple Watch Heart Rate Tracking Upgraded

Apple is set to greatly improve heart-rate tracking on the Apple Watch with the upcoming watchOS 27 update. This enhancement will provide more accurate and reliable data.

The update aligns with Apple's ongoing efforts to bolster the health and fitness capabilities of its wearable devices. The company has consistently emphasized health and fitness in its products, particularly with the Apple Watch.

## The AI Health Coach: A Delayed Launch

For over a year, rumors have circulated about Project Mulberry, Apple's AI-powered health coach initiative. According to Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, the features are still on track for iOS 27, although they may not be released until later in the cycle. The delayed release may disappoint some, as users had come to expect a more comprehensive health and wellness offering from Apple.

## A History of Health Focus on Apple Devices

Since its launch, the Apple Watch has included various health-related features, such as heart rate monitoring, electrocardiogram (ECG) capabilities, and fall detection. The company's focus on health is likely to continue, with potential future updates and features that leverage AI and machine learning. Apple's investments in health research and development have led to numerous innovations, including the detection of irregular heart rhythms and alerts for high or low heart rates.
