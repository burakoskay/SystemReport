## The Rise of 'Subliminals'

In the world of self-improvement, a new trend has emerged: 'subliminals.' These YouTube and TikTok videos promise to transform listeners' lives, including their physical appearance, through ASMR affirmations. For young women, in particular, these videos have become a go-to for those seeking a 'glow-up.'

The concept is simple: by listening to soothing affirmations, often accompanied by calming sounds or music, listeners can rewire their subconscious mind to believe in their own beauty and potential. The videos often feature gentle whispers, tapping, or crinkling sounds, designed to induce a state of relaxation and heightened suggestibility.
