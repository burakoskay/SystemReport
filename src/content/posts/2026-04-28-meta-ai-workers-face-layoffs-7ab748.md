## Introduction to Meta's AI Workers
More than 700 workers contracted to train Meta's AI systems in Ireland are at risk of losing their jobs, according to documents. These workers play a crucial role in the development of Meta's AI, as they are responsible for labeling and categorizing data that helps to improve the accuracy of the AI models.

## The Role of Contractors in AI Development
Contractors are used by Meta to train its AI systems. Documents show that these workers may soon be laid off. The use of contractors for AI development is a common practice in the tech industry, as it allows companies to quickly scale up or down to meet changing demands. However, this practice can also lead to uncertainty and instability for the workers involved.

## The Impact of Layoffs on Workers
The layoffs could affect over 700 workers in Ireland. Meta has not commented on the situation. The potential layoffs have significant implications for the workers and their families, who may be facing financial uncertainty and stress. The impact of the layoffs could also be felt in the broader community, as the loss of jobs can have a ripple effect on local businesses and the economy.
