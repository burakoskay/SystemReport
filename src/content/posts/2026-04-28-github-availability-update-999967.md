## History of GitHub

GitHub was founded in 2008 by Tom Preston-Werner, Chris Wanstrath, and PJ Hyett. Since its inception, the platform has grown rapidly, becoming one of the largest communities of developers in the world. In 2018, Microsoft acquired GitHub for $7.5 billion, further expanding its reach and resources. The acquisition has enabled GitHub to leverage Microsoft's expertise in cloud computing and artificial intelligence, enhancing its platform and services.
