# Musk v Altman Trial Exposes OpenAI's Corporate Past
The trial between Elon Musk and Sam Altman has entered its third week, with the plaintiffs and defense resting their cases. The trial has exposed details about OpenAI's corporate past, including a five-day saga in 2023 when Altman was effectively fired then rehired.
## The Case Against Altman
Musk alleges that OpenAI and Altman breached the company's charitable trust by shifting from a non-profit to a for-profit structure. The trial has featured testimony from former executives, including Mira Murati, who accused Altman of "creating chaos" at the company. Murati testified that Altman had a pattern of "saying one thing to one person and completely the opposite to another person".
## History of OpenAI
OpenAI was founded in 2015 by Musk, Altman, and Greg Brockman. The company's early days were marked by a focus on artificial intelligence research, but it has since shifted to a more commercial approach. The trial has highlighted the tensions between Musk and Altman, with Musk alleging that Altman misled him about the company's intentions.
