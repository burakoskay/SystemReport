## Pothole Detection via AI

Samsara, a fleet management company, has developed an AI model to detect and assess the deterioration of potholes. This technology provides accurate and timely data on road conditions. The AI model uses data from connected cars to identify different types of potholes and gauge their deterioration rate. Municipalities and private companies can now approach road maintenance more efficiently and targeted.

The integration of AI in fleet management and road maintenance could have significant implications for the industry, from improving road safety to reducing maintenance costs. For instance, Samsara's AI model can help municipalities prioritize repairs and prevent further damage to roads and vehicles. This proactive approach can save municipalities and companies substantial amounts of money in the long run.
