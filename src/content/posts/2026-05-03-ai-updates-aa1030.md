## Living Product Specs with Specsight
Specsight is a tool that generates living product specs from your codebase. Your codebase already knows what the product does. Now everyone else can too. Specsight writes the spec, and everyone stays on the same page. Context, Action, Outcome — three columns that tell your whole team what the product does. Every time your team ships a release, Specsight detects what changed and updates the spec.
