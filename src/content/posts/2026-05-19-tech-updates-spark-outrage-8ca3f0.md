## Introduction to the Latest Tech Updates

Instagram's new feature has sparked widespread outrage among its users. The update, which changes the way users scroll through their feed, has been greeted with near-unanimous negative feedback. Users are now forced to scroll through posts side-to-side, rather than the traditional up-and-down movement.

The update appears to be an attempt to mimic the success of Instagram's stories feature, which has become a huge hit among users. However, the new feature has been met with resistance, with many users taking to social media to express their frustration.

## The Impact of the Update on Users

The update has been rolled out to a huge number of Instagram users at once, with many reporting that they have been forced to adapt to the new feature without warning. The lack of a clear option to reverse the change has added to the frustration, with many users feeling that their experience has been disrupted.

The backlash against the update has been so severe that Instagram's boss has been forced to claim that the feature was rolled out by mistake. However, it remains to be seen whether the company will listen to user feedback and revert to the old format.

In other news, WhatsApp is testing a new feature that allows users to send disappearing messages. The feature, which is currently available in the beta version of the app, allows users to set a time limit on their messages, after which they will be deleted.
