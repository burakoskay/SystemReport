## Patina Secures $2 Million in Funding

Patina, a fragrance tech company, announced Thursday a $2 million raise from investors, including Betaworks and True Ventures. This investment marks a significant bet on a startup looking to disrupt an industry that has seen little innovation in decades.

## The Fragrance Industry's Funding Landscape

The funding round is notable not just for the amount, but for the investors involved. Betaworks and True Ventures are well-known for backing companies that challenge traditional industries. Their investment in Patina signals a growing interest in the fragrance tech space, which has historically been slow to adopt new technologies.

The fragrance industry has remained largely unchanged for almost half a century. Traditional players in the market have focused on established business models, with little emphasis on innovation or technological advancement. This has created an opportunity for new entrants like Patina to shake up the market with fresh approaches and modern technologies.

Fragrance creation involves a complex process of blending essential oils, aroma compounds, and other ingredients to create a unique scent.
