# New Home Screen Design
Roku unveiled its biggest Home Screen redesign in over a decade. The redesign features a 'Top Picks' row at the top, which suggests content based on the user's viewing history and preferences.

# AI-Powered Features
The new Home Screen includes AI to organize apps. The 'Top Picks' row uses AI to suggest content. This emphasis on AI-powered features positions Roku as a major player in the smart TV market, where competitors such as Google TV and Amazon Fire TV are also investing heavily in AI-driven recommendations.

# Top Picks Row
The 'Top Picks' row suggests content based on the user's viewing history and preferences. This row is designed to help users discover new content and easily access their favorite shows and movies. By prioritizing user experience, Roku aims to increase user engagement and retention on its platform.
