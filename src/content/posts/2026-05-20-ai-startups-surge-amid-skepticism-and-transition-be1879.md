## Asm.js Phasing Out in Web Development

Mozilla’s SpiderMonkey blog announced Asm.js deprecation by May 2026. The post cites performance bottlenecks and the rise of WebAssembly as primary drivers. Developer communities on Hacker News (HN) have reacted with 117 upvotes and 56 comments, most debating migration paths.

Critics argue Asm.js’s demise accelerates the web’s reliance on compiled code. Proponents counter that WebAssembly’s 2023 performance benchmarks justify the transition. The post provides no timeline for final Asm.js support removal, but notes Firefox 129 (expected 2025) will begin deprecation warnings.
