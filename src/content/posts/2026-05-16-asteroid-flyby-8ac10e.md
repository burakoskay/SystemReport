## Introduction to Asteroid Apophis

Asteroid Apophis, a 1,100-foot-wide near-Earth asteroid, is set to fly by Earth in 2029. This event presents a rare opportunity for scientists to study the asteroid up close and gather valuable data on its composition and structure.

The asteroid's close approach will allow scientists to observe it with both optical and radar telescopes, potentially revealing surface details as small as a few meters. This information is crucial for understanding the asteroid's interior and developing effective plans for deflecting similar asteroids in the future.
