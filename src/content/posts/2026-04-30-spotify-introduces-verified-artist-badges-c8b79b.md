## Introduction to Verified Artist Badges
Spotify's new "Verified by Spotify" badge helps listeners distinguish human artists from AI-generated ones. The badge requires artists to have an identifiable presence both on and off the platform, including concert dates, merchandise, and linked social media profiles.
## Criteria for Verification
The verification criteria include an artist's online presence and activity. Spotify looks for artists with a clear presence both on and off the platform. This includes having a website, social media profiles, and a history of live performances. By having these elements, artists can demonstrate their authenticity and qualify for the "Verified by Spotify" badge.
