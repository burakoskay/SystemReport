## Pursuit Lands $22M for Gov Sales Push

On Wednesday, Pursuit announced a $22 million Series A funding round led by Mike Rosengarten, co-founder of OpenGov. The round included participation from notable venture capital firms, as well as backers Bill Gurley and Jack Altman.

Pursuit's platform helps businesses identify and pursue government contracts. The company aims to simplify the process of selling to government agencies, which can be complex and time-consuming.

The funding round highlights growing interest in government technology (gov tech). Gov tech companies provide solutions for government agencies, driven by the need for modernization and efficiency in public sector operations.
