## Introduction to Clouted
Clouted, a startup focused on video clipping, has raised $7 million in a seed round led by Slow Ventures. The funding round indicates investor confidence in Clouted's mission to help make short videos go viral. This investment comes at a critical juncture for the company as it seeks to capitalize on the growing demand for short-form video content.

## The Funding
The $7 million seed round is led by Slow Ventures. This investment aims to support Clouted's goal of simplifying the process of creating viral short videos. The funding round suggests that investors believe in the potential of Clouted's technology to disrupt the video editing and content creation tool market.

## The Company
Clouted operates in the video clipping space, which involves extracting and editing specific parts of longer videos to create shorter, more engaging clips. This process is crucial for content creators and marketers looking to maximize the impact of their video content on social media platforms. The company's technology likely involves advanced algorithms for video analysis and editing, enabling users to easily identify and extract the most engaging parts of longer videos.
