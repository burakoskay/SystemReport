## SynthID Watermarking Gains Traction

Google's SynthID, a tool to distinguish truth from fiction in AI-generated content, is now integrated into OpenAI's and Nvidia's AI offerings. This move enables the detection of synthetic content across competing generative models.

SynthID stamps each piece of generated content with an invisible identifier that can be detected by downstream tools. The adoption signals a coordinated effort to create a shared detection standard.

The integration with OpenAI and Nvidia expands SynthID's reach beyond Google's own products, indicating a growing recognition of the need for content provenance. As AI-generated content becomes increasingly prevalent, the ability to identify synthetic content will be crucial for maintaining trust in digital media.

The development of SynthID and its adoption by major AI players highlights the industry's acknowledgment of the challenges posed by AI-generated content. With the proliferation of AI-generated content, the risk of misinformation and disinformation increases. SynthID's technology offers a potential solution to this problem.

## Ocean Raises $28M to Combat AI-Driven Phishing

Ocean, an agentic email security platform founded by a former teen hacker and Iron Dome researcher, secured a $28 million Series A round led by Lightspeed Venture Partners. The funding will fuel the rollout of its AI-powered phishing defense.

The platform identifies and blocks AI-generated phishing attempts. ## Open-Source LLM Extension Brings AI Agents to Google Sheets

A developer released a free, open-source extension that lets any large language model operate inside Google Sheets. The project lists five providers and includes reasoning & thinking models.

The extension packs ten specialized skills and self-corrects when a formula reference fails. Users can type a single command and watch three columns fill in one conversation turn. The tool stores session memory and builds a semantic search index.

Notably, the extension allows users to pay providers directly, with no markup. Security features include bringing-your-own API keys. This approach gives users more control over their data and the models they use.

The extension's capabilities and features demonstrate the potential for AI-powered productivity tools to enhance user experience and efficiency. By integrating large language models into Google Sheets, users can automate complex tasks and gain insights from their data.

## Technical Mechanics: How SynthID Works

SynthID uses a technique called watermarking to identify AI-generated content. The tool stamps each piece of generated content with an invisible identifier that can be detected by downstream tools. This allows for the detection of synthetic content across competing generative models.

The watermarking technique used by SynthID is designed to be robust and resistant to tampering. This ensures that the identifier remains intact even if the content is modified or manipulated.

The technical mechanics of SynthID's watermarking technique are crucial to its effectiveness. By providing a reliable method for detecting AI-generated content, SynthID enables the development of more sophisticated AI-powered tools and applications.
