## OSHA Probes SpaceX Starbase Worker Death

The Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) is investigating a worker death at SpaceX's Starbase facility. The site has a higher injury rate than all other SpaceX facilities.

## Injury Rates and Investigation Details

The Starbase facility's injury rate exceeds that of other SpaceX sites, according to available information. OSHA's investigation aims to determine the cause of the worker's death and identify potential safety violations.
