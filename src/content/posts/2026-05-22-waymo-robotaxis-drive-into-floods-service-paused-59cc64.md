## Waymo's Flooded Roads Problem

Waymo's robotaxi service is suspended in four cities after its vehicles encountered flooded roads. A robotaxi got stuck in a flooded street in Atlanta, Georgia, for about an hour. The vehicle was recovered and removed from the scene, according to Waymo. This incident occurred despite the company's efforts to address the issue through a software recall.

## The Technical Challenge

The current software update may not be sufficient to handle all flood-related scenarios. A robotaxi still entered a flooded intersection in Atlanta after a storm produced significant rainfall before a National Weather Service warning. Waymo issued a software recall last week with precautions to avoid flooded areas, but the incident indicates that further improvements are needed.
