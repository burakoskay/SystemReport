## Introduction to Android 17
Google's Android 17 launches this summer with features like Gemini Intelligence and customizable widgets. The 'Create My Widget' feature lets users generate their own widgets or ask Gemini to finish a booking in Chrome on Android.

The 'Create My Widget' feature will first launch on the latest Samsung Galaxy and Google Pixel phones this summer. Users can create custom widgets tailored to their needs and interests. For example, a user could create a widget that displays their favorite news feed or the current weather.

## Gemini Intelligence
Gemini Intelligence is an AI-powered tool that lets users perform tasks like booking flights or sending emails. Gemini will be integrated into Chrome, allowing users to access its features from within the browser. A device with at least 4GB of RAM is required to use the software.

Gemini's integration into Chrome is significant, as it lets users access the tool from anywhere within the browser. Users can use Gemini to perform tasks like booking flights or sending emails without leaving the browser.
