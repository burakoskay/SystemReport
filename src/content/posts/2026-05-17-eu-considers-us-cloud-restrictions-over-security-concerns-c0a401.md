The European Union is considering restricting the use of US cloud platforms, such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, to process sensitive government data due to security concerns.

The EU's concern stems from the potential risks associated with relying on foreign-based cloud services. According to reports, the European Commission is examining the use of US cloud platforms by EU governments and institutions.
