## Introduction to Tappy
The Tappy is a tiny two-button keyboard that connects to various devices over Bluetooth, offering more functionality than just turning the page. It can be used to scroll vertical content in browsers or social media apps and skip to the next or previous track when listening to music or audiobooks.

## Design and Functionality
The Tappy is a compact device with two buttons, designed for easy use and navigation. Its ability to scroll vertical content and control music and audiobooks makes it a useful tool for reading and listening on the go. For instance, users can utilize the Tappy to navigate through their social media feeds or browse the web with ease, all while keeping their hands free from their devices.

## Comparison to Kobo's Remote
The Kobo remote offers page-turning functionality, but the Tappy's compatibility with multiple devices over Bluetooth sets it apart. The Tappy can connect to devices beyond Boox hardware, making it a more versatile option. This compatibility is particularly useful for users who own multiple devices and want a single remote to control them all.
