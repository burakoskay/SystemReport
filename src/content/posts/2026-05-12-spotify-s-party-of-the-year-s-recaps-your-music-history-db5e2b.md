Spotify's new feature, Party of the Year(s), is a Wrapped-style recap of your entire listening history, showing the number of unique songs you've listened to. According to TechCrunch, this feature will show users the number of unique songs they've listened to until now.

Spotify's Wrapped feature was first introduced as a way to summarize users' yearly listening habits. The feature was well-received by users, who enjoyed looking back on their favorite songs and artists from the past year. Party of the Year(s) expands this concept, providing users with a recap of their entire streaming history.
