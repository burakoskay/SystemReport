## NYC Mayor Takes to Twitch

New York City Mayor Zohran Mamdani launches a Twitch series today at 4 p.m. ET. The series aims to chat with New Yorkers. This move marks a new approach to public engagement, leveraging a platform primarily known for gaming to foster direct communication between the mayor and the citizens.

## The Tech Behind the Stream

Twitch allows for real-time interaction between streamers and their audience through live chat. This feature will enable Mayor Mamdani to engage directly with the public, responding to comments and questions in real-time. The platform's infrastructure is designed to handle a high volume of concurrent users, making it a suitable choice for a city of New York's size.
