## A New Way to Discover Content

Netflix has started rolling out a redesign of its iPhone app today, as previously announced, with a focus on helping users discover new content. The streaming giant has introduced 'Clips,' a TikTok-like vertical video feed that shares highlights from its original programming. This new feature aims to make it easier for users to stumble upon new shows and movies. The Clips feature is available within the Netflix mobile app, allowing users to browse through a curated feed of vertical videos. These videos are short, snappy, and designed to grab the user's attention.

## How Clips Works

The feed will showcase a variety of content, from action-packed movie scenes to emotional drama moments. According to the source, Clips is part of an announced overhaul of the mobile app. Netflix's move into vertical video is a strategic attempt to compete with platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels, and offer a unique experience that combines entertainment with discovery.
