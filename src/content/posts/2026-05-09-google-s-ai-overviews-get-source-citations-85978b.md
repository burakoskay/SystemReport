## Google's AI Overviews Get Source Citations

Google's AI search feature, known as AI Overviews, will start citing its sources in several new ways. The update aims to improve transparency and credibility. This change comes as Google faces criticism for not providing enough context and sources for its AI-generated responses.

## What's Changing

The new feature will allow users to see where the information comes from. Google will add links to sources directly within the AI-generated summaries. For instance, if a user asks about a recent scientific breakthrough, the AI Overview might include a link to the original research paper or a reputable news article. This increased transparency addresses concerns about the reliability and trustworthiness of AI-generated information.

## Why It Matters

The update is significant because it provides users with a clear way to evaluate the credibility of the information they receive. By providing clear citations, Google aims to help users assess the accuracy and relevance of the information. This is particularly important in areas like science, technology, and healthcare, where accuracy and credibility are paramount.
