## Spirit Airlines Grounds All Flights Amidst Soaring Jet Fuel Prices

Spirit Airlines, an ultra-low-cost air carrier, abruptly shut down operations on Saturday morning, canceling all flights and leaving passengers stranded. The airline's website now redirects to spiritrestructuring.com, advising fliers not to go to airports.

The shutdown comes after 34 years in business, with Spirit Airlines citing rising jet fuel prices as a major factor. The prices of jet fuel have doubled since Trump's war on Iran, making it increasingly difficult for airlines to maintain profitability. On Saturday morning at 3 AM ET, air traffic control records show controllers and pilots signing off to each other as the last flights came in for landings.
