## The Science Behind Temporal Difference Learning

Reinforcement learning is one of the oldest and most powerful ideas linking neuroscience and AI. In the late 1980s, computer science researchers developed algorithms that could learn how to perform complex behaviors on their own, using only rewards and punishments as a teaching signal. Temporal difference learning, a key component of reinforcement learning, allows AI systems to learn from experience and make predictions about future outcomes. This process is remarkably similar to how humans learn and adapt to their environment.
