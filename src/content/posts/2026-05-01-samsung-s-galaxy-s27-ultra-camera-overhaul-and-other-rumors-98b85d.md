## Samsung Galaxy S27 Ultra Camera Rumored to Get Major Overhaul

The Samsung Galaxy S27 Ultra is rumored to get a significant camera upgrade, potentially ditching its 3x telephoto lens. This move could mark a substantial change in Samsung's approach to smartphone photography.

## Details on the Galaxy S27 Ultra Camera

While specific details on the new camera setup are scarce, the rumor suggests that Samsung is looking to revamp its camera system. This could involve new sensor technologies or lens designs. The current Galaxy S26 Ultra features a quad-camera setup, including a 200MP primary sensor, a 40MP front camera, and support for 8K video recording at 30fps.

## Other Samsung Rumors: Galaxy Book Laptops and Galaxy A27

In addition to the Galaxy S27 Ultra, Samsung is reportedly preparing new Galaxy Book laptops running Android with One UI 9 skin. This move could signal a shift in Samsung's laptop strategy, potentially offering more Android-based options.

The Galaxy A27 has emerged in detailed renders, showcasing a design similar to its predecessors, the A37 and A57. The device is expected to feature a 6.7-inch AMOLED display, a 50MP main camera, an 8MP ultrawide camera, and a 2MP macro camera. It will likely be powered by the Snapdragon 6 Gen 3 chip and come with 6GB of RAM.

## Galaxy Z Flip 8 Rumors: No Battery Upgrade

The Galaxy Z Flip 8 might not see a battery upgrade, with rumors suggesting that it will have a similar battery capacity to its predecessor, the Flip 7. The Flip 7 features a 4,300mAh battery, and the rumored specs for the Flip 8, including batteries EB-BF776 and EB-BF777, point to a similar capacity.
