## A New Era in Cybersecurity

Anthropic's powerful AI model, Mythos, has been making waves in the cybersecurity community with its unprecedented capabilities. The model, which can find and exploit software vulnerabilities, has been touted as a game-changer in the field. But what does this mean for the future of cybersecurity?

## The Asymmetry of Cybersecurity

For years, cybersecurity has been plagued by an asymmetry: defenders must find all vulnerabilities in their code to make it secure, while attackers only need to find one to launch an attack. This has led to a cat-and-mouse game where attackers always seem to have the upper hand. However, AI tools like Mythos suggest a possible alternative: what if finding every vulnerability in a piece of software were just as fast and easy as finding a few of them, thanks to automation?

## The Potential of Mythos

Mythos has the potential to radically shift the balance in favor of defenders. By automating the process of vulnerability discovery and exploitation, Mythos could enable defenders to comprehensively catalog and patch vulnerabilities prior to the release of software. This could mean that attackers and defenders would be on an equal footing when it comes to finding vulnerabilities.

Mythos uses a combination of machine learning algorithms and natural language processing to identify vulnerabilities in software.
