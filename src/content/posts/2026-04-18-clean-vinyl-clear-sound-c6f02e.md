## Cleaning Your Vinyl Records
Dirt and grime on vinyl records cause 75% of playback issues. Vacuum, ultrasonic, solution, and brush methods can remove debris.
## Methods for Cleaning
The vacuum method uses gentle suction to remove surface dirt. Ultrasonic cleaning uses high-frequency sound waves to dislodge particles. Solution methods involve applying a cleaning fluid to the record's surface. The brush method uses soft-bristled brushes to sweep away debris.
## Preserving Sound Quality
Proper cleaning reduces clicks and pops by 90%. Regular cleaning extends record life and maintains sound quality.
