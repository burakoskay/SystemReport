## OpenRouter's Valuation Skyrockets

OpenRouter has raised $113 million in a Series B funding round led by CapitalG, more than doubling its valuation to $1.3B in just a year. The company's usage has grown 5x over the past six months. This rapid growth indicates a significant shift towards multi-AI-model platforms.

## The Funding Round

The Series B funding round led by CapitalG brings OpenRouter's total investment to date to an undisclosed amount. The exact terms of the investment weren't disclosed. However, it's clear that investors are betting big on OpenRouter's vision of a unified AI model platform. This funding round is a testament to the confidence investors have in OpenRouter's approach to providing access to multiple AI models through a single platform.

## What's Driving Growth?

OpenRouter allows users to access AI models through a single platform. This approach enables developers to build applications that leverage the strengths of multiple AI models. By streamlining access to AI capabilities, OpenRouter is making it easier for developers to create sophisticated AI-powered products. Under the hood, OpenRouter's platform relies on a combination of containerization and API management to provide seamless access to multiple AI models.
