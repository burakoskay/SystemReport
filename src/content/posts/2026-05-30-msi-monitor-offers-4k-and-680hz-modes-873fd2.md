## Technical Details
The monitor has two preset resolution-refresh combos. MSI highlighted a 4K mode for *Crimson Desert* and a 680Hz mode for *Counter-Strike 2*. The 4K resolution offers a high level of detail, while the 680Hz refresh rate provides smoother motion.

The 4K mode promises sharper textures and richer scenery for *Crimson Desert*. This can enhance the gaming experience, especially in games that require attention to detail.
