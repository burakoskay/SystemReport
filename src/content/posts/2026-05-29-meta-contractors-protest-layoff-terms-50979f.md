## Meta Contractors Protest Layoff Terms

Meta contractors set to be laid off at the company's European headquarters claim they will receive less generous severance packages than their full-time colleagues. The contractors say they will get a smaller amount of severance pay and fewer benefits. This disparity has sparked protests among the contractors, who feel they are being treated unfairly.

## Dispute Over Severance Packages

The contractors assert that this disparity is unfair. A review of their situation reveals that full-time employees receive more comprehensive severance packages. For instance, full-time employees are reportedly entitled to several months of pay, continued health insurance, and outplacement support. In contrast, contractors claim they will only receive a fraction of the severance pay and limited benefits.
