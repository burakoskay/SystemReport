## China's Tiangong Space Station Achieves Artificial Photosynthesis Breakthrough

Chinese astronauts on board the Tiangong space station have successfully demonstrated a new way of making rocket fuel products and breathable oxygen by mimicking a chemical reaction in plants. This breakthrough technology could be utilized in China's planned moon base.

The artificial photosynthesis technology, which has been in development since 2015, converts carbon dioxide and water into oxygen and rocket fuel ingredients using a simple "drawer-like" apparatus and a "semiconductor catalyst."
