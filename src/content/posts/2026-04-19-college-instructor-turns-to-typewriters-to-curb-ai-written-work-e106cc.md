# The Typewriter Uprising

A Colorado college instructor has taken an unconventional approach to curb AI-written work: assigning essays on typewriters. The goal is twofold — to teach students life skills and to ensure academic integrity in an era where AI tools can generate essays with ease.

## The Problem with AI in Education

The increasing prevalence of AI tools in education has raised concerns about academic integrity. While some students use these tools to enhance their learning experience, others rely on them to complete assignments. This has led to instances of students submitting AI-generated work as their own.

The issue is not just about cheating; it's also about the potential for AI to hinder learning. When students rely on AI tools to complete assignments, they may not fully understand the material. This can lead to a shallow understanding of complex concepts and a lack of critical thinking skills.

## Teaching Life Skills with Typewriters

The instructor's decision to use typewriters is a deliberate attempt to teach students life skills that are often overlooked in the digital age. By using typewriters, students are forced to slow down and think more critically about their writing. They must also learn to navigate the physical limitations of a typewriter, which can be a valuable lesson in itself.

The use of typewriters also serves as a reminder that technology is not always the answer. In an era where AI tools are increasingly prevalent, it's essential to recognize the value of human intuition and creativity.

## The Future of Education and AI

As AI continues to play a larger role in education, it's essential to consider the implications of relying on these tools. While AI can be a powerful tool for learning, it's crucial to ensure that students are not relying on it to complete assignments.

The instructor's approach may seem old-fashioned, but it's a reminder that there are still valuable lessons to be learned from traditional methods. As educators, it's essential to strike a balance between technology and human intuition.

## What to Watch

The debate surrounding AI in education is far from over. As AI tools continue to evolve, it's essential to consider the implications of relying on them. Will educators find new ways to adapt to the changing landscape, or will they revert to traditional methods? One thing is certain — the future of education will be shaped by the decisions made today.
