## Technical Mechanics

The orbiter's design is based on a hybrid spacecraft architecture, which combines the benefits of a traditional monolithic spacecraft with the flexibility of a modular design. The orbiter's modular design allows for easier integration of new instruments and enables more efficient use of resources.
