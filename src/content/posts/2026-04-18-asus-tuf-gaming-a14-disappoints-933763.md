## Introduction
The Asus TUF Gaming A14 ditches the discrete graphics card, relying on AMD's silicon for graphics processing. This approach yields unimpressive results.
## The Hardware
The TUF Gaming A14's performance is decent, with AMD's new silicon handling graphics. However, the laptop fails to fully utilize this technology, leaving gamers wanting more.
## Conclusion
The TUF Gaming A14's shortcomings stem from poor design optimization. With better design, this laptop could have delivered more. Instead, it delivers mid-tier performance, neither excelling nor offending.
