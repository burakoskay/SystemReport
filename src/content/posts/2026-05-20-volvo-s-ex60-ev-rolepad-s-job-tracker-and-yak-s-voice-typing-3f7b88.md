## Rolepad: A Job Application Tracker with Company Reviews

Rolepad is a free tool that helps job seekers stay organized. It allows users to capture every job application, record company facts, and track interview stages. The platform also connects candidates with hiring teams and provides a diagram of their job search journey. Rolepad includes company reviews and a recruiter autoresponder. This tool is particularly useful for job seekers who apply to multiple positions simultaneously, as it helps them keep track of various applications and interactions with hiring teams. By streamlining the job application process, Rolepad aims to reduce the stress and complexity associated with job hunting.

## Yak: Voice Typing Made Easy

Yak is a voice typing tool built with Tauri and Rust. It auto-presses Enter and turns voice into polished text. Speaking is 4x faster with Yak, saving users 1 day per week. The tool offers a 30-day Pro trial with no credit card required. Yak's features include filler word removal and self-correction elimination, and it adapts to users' vocabulary and style. This tool has the potential to significantly increase productivity for individuals who rely heavily on written communication, such as writers, developers, and professionals. Yak's ability to produce high-quality text quickly can be a game-changer for those who spend a lot of time typing.
