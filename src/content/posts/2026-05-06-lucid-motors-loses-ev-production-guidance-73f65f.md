## Lucid Motors Pulls 2024 EV Production Guidance

Lucid Motors has pulled its 2024 production guidance, citing swelling inventory and ongoing cost-cutting measures. This move signals challenges for the EV manufacturer as it navigates a shifting market.

The company, known for its high-end electric vehicles, had previously set expectations for a certain number of EVs to be produced in 2024. However, in a recent update, Lucid Motors announced that it would no longer provide this guidance. The decision comes as the company faces increased inventory levels and implements cost-cutting measures across the organization.
