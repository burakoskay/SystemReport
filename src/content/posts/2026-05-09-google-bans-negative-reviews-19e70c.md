## Introduction to Google's Review Policy Update

Google has taken a significant step in maintaining the integrity of its review system by banning people from posting negative reviews of their former employers. This move is part of an effort to ensure that reviews on Google My Business are honest and unbiased, providing users with a more accurate representation of businesses.

The update to Google's review policies comes after concerns were raised about the potential for former employees to post biased reviews, which could harm a company's reputation. By prohibiting negative reviews from former employees, Google aims to create a more trustworthy review system that benefits both businesses and consumers.

## Background on Google My Business Reviews

Google My Business is a tool that allows businesses to manage their online presence across Google, including Google Maps and Search.
