DeepSeek's flagship model now costs 75% less, with the company framing the change as a permanent pricing tier. This move is described in a Bloomberg report dated May 23, 2026. DeepSeek has also introduced Reasonix, a native coding agent designed for high caching efficiency and lower operating costs.
## DeepSeek’s Pricing Change
The company has cut the usage fees of its flagship model by 75%. This change is not a limited-time promotion but a permanent pricing adjustment. According to Bloomberg, the percentage reduction signals a substantial shift in revenue expectations.
## Impact of the Discount
A 75% reduction in price can make marginally viable workloads cost-effective. Startups running inference at scale can expect lower cloud spending and faster iteration cycles. This pricing adjustment will likely have a significant impact on the industry, as companies will be able to allocate more resources to other areas of their business.
## Reasonix: A Native Coding Agent
Reasonix is described as a native coding agent with high caching and low cost. It stores intermediate computation results, reducing redundant processing during code generation tasks. High caching reduces the number of model invocations needed, directly cutting costs under DeepSeek's usage-based pricing. The introduction of Reasonix demonstrates DeepSeek's commitment to providing efficient solutions for its customers.
