## SAP's $1.16B AI Bet

SAP is investing $1.16 billion in Prior Labs, an 18-month-old German AI startup. This significant investment signals SAP's strategic focus on artificial intelligence.

## Details of the Deal

The deal includes SAP's plan to acquire Prior Labs. SAP also announced restrictions on customer agents' use of AI models, allowing only select models like Nvidia's NemoClaw.
