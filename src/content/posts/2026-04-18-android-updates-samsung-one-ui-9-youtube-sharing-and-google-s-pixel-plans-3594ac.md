Samsung is working on its next major update, One UI 9, expected to launch later this summer. A recent leak reveals two exciting features: 'Tap to Share' and Bixby widgets on the home screen. This update aims to enhance user experience with new functionality.

YouTube is overhauling its video sharing feature, discontinuing 'Clips' and introducing a new 'share at timestamp' option on phones. This change makes it easier for users to share specific moments from videos.

Google is exploring new hardware and software innovations, including a Pixel 'laptop' and 'Pixel Glow' lights, which might also be integrated into phones. Android 17 brings a touch of Pixel Watch whimsy to the notification shade on Pixel phones, as seen in the latest beta release.

Amazon has launched its newest Fire TV Stick, which runs on Vega OS, marking the second device to do so. The company plans to ditch Android on all future Fire TV Sticks. This move marks a new direction for Amazon's streaming devices.

In the world of deals, Samsung's Galaxy S26 Ultra and new Galaxy Buds 4 Pro are available at a discounted price of $325 off. The white Galaxy S26 Ultra is also on sale at $250 off on Amazon. Google's Pixel 10 is available at up to $354 off. These offers, along with other tech deals, make it an exciting time for consumers.

The tech industry is witnessing significant changes, with major players like Samsung, Google, and Amazon driving innovation. Users can look forward to enhanced experiences and new possibilities.
