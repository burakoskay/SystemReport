## Introduction to Recent Tech Developments
Amazon's Alexa+ can now generate custom AI podcasts on demand. This move expands Amazon's assistant into a personalized AI content platform. Starlink, on the other hand, is raising prices across its satellite internet plans in the US.

## Price Hikes and New Features
The cheapest 100Mbps Residential plan from Starlink will now cost $55/month, up from $50. The 200Mbps residential plan has jumped from $80 to $85/month, while the Residential Max plan increased from $120 to $130/month. Standby Mode, which allows subscribers to pause their main Starlink service while using unlimited low-speed data, now costs $10/month instead of $5. These price hikes may impact the affordability of satellite internet for many users.

Google has been rolling out updates to its apps, including a redesign of shortcuts in Gboard and an update to the Find Hub app that lets users add location sharing notifications. The company is also beginning to roll out its gradient icon redesign for Workspace apps. These updates aim to improve the user experience and provide more features to Google's vast user base.
