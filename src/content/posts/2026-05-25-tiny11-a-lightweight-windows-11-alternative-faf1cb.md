## A Breath of Fresh Air for Old Machines

If you're stuck with an old computer that can't handle the demands of Windows 11, there's a new alternative: Tiny11. This lightweight version of Microsoft's operating system is designed to work on a wide range of computers, giving users a chance to breathe new life into their aging machines.

Tiny11 is not just a stripped-down version of Windows 11; it's a carefully crafted operating system that aims to provide a seamless user experience without the bloat. It's perfect for those who can't or don't want to upgrade to the full version of Windows 11.

The Dutch Ministry of Justice and Security investigated the privacy risks related to Microsoft Windows 10 Enterprise, Office 365 ProPlus, and Office Online. The results showed that Microsoft has made efforts to mitigate these risks, but there's still work to be done.

## The Privacy Concerns with Windows

Microsoft has implemented various measures to reduce privacy risks, such as adapting its Data Viewer Tool to display Office 365 ProPlus telemetry data. The company has also offered a choice for system administrators to minimize the telemetry level. However, these improvements are not yet available in Office Online and the mobile Office apps.

For enterprises, negotiating with Microsoft to implement similar privacy guarantees is crucial. The European Data Protection Supervisor's ongoing investigation into Microsoft's contract terms can serve as a reference point. Individuals can also take steps to minimize their data collection by opting for the lowest possible level of data collection in Windows 10, namely Security.
