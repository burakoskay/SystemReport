## Introduction to Apple's AI Update
Apple will allow users to choose their preferred AI model in iOS 27. This update will enable third-party chatbots to power Apple's AI features system-wide. According to Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, compatible third-party AI models will be able to run Siri and other Apple Intelligence features like Writing Tools and Image Playground.

## Concrete Details of the Update
The update is expected to be released this fall for iOS 27, iPadOS 27, and macOS 27. Users will also be able to choose different Siri voices for different AI models. Apple's move to open up its AI features to third-party models is a significant development in the tech industry.
