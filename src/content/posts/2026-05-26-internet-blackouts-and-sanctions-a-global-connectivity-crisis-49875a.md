## The Impact of Sanctions on Content Delivery Networks

CDNs generally help internet performance by keeping traffic contained within a geographic area or network. They also provide security services, such as combating DDoS attacks. Some CDNs provide DNS over HTTPS (DoH) resolution services. For instance, Cloudflare offers a DoH service that encrypts DNS requests and provides a secure way to access websites. The relationship between CDNs and sanctions is complex. On one hand, CDNs can help bypass sanctions by providing access to content from other regions. On the other hand, CDNs may be required to comply with sanctions by blocking access to certain websites or services.
