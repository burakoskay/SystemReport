Apple's Digital ID feature in the Wallet app now supports age verification in several cases on iPhone. This feature allows users to verify their age when purchasing age-restricted products or services. The Digital ID feature is currently supported in several states and with participating businesses. For instance, users can use their Digital ID to verify their age when buying age-restricted items.

The expansion of Digital ID to include age verification is a significant development, as it provides users with a convenient and secure way to prove their age. ## HCB Mobile: Financial Inclusion for Teenagers

Mohamad, a 17-year-old developer, created a mobile app called HCB Mobile. The app supports the financial needs of teenager-led nonprofits, clubs, and hackathons. HCB provides a financial backbone for over 6,500 organizations, offering 501(c)(3) nonprofit status, access to a bank account, a donation collection platform, and debit cards. The HCB Mobile app, built using Expo, a React Native framework, allows users to manage their organization's finances directly from their pocket.

The app's features, such as mobile tap-to-pay terminal provisioning and push provisioning, provide a seamless experience for users. Mohamad optimized the app's performance using techniques like memoization and component recycling.
