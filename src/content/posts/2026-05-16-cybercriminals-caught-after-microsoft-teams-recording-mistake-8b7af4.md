## Cybercriminals Caught After Microsoft Teams Recording Mistake
Two cybercriminal twins were caught by authorities after they forgot to turn off a Microsoft Teams recording feature during one of their operations. This mistake led to their identification and subsequent arrest.

The twins, whose names have not been disclosed, were allegedly involved in various cybercrimes. Their use of Microsoft Teams for communication and collaboration included enabling the recording feature, which captured incriminating evidence. This evidence was crucial in building a case against them.
