## Rivian Introduces AI-Powered Voice Assistant

Rivian is rolling out an AI-powered voice assistant for its vehicles. The feature will be available on all Rivian Gen 1 and Gen 2 vehicles, as well as on the R2 when it launches. This move marks a significant step in Rivian's efforts to enhance the in-car experience.

The voice assistant is designed to provide a seamless user experience, allowing drivers to control various functions with voice commands. This technology has the potential to greatly improve the driving experience, making it safer and more convenient.

## Amazon Expands Delivery Options

Amazon is rolling out its new 30-minute delivery option in dozens of locations across the US. The service, known as Amazon Now, aims to provide customers with fast and convenient delivery options. This expansion is part of Amazon's broader efforts to enhance its logistics and delivery capabilities.

The rise of same-day delivery services has increased competition in the e-commerce market. Amazon's move is likely a response to this trend, as the company seeks to remain competitive. With its vast resources and infrastructure, Amazon is well-positioned to make a significant impact in this space.
