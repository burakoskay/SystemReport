## QuTwo's Big Raise

QuTwo, a Finnish AI lab founded by former AMD Silo AI CEO Peter Sarlin, has reached a valuation of $380 million after securing a $29 million angel round. The funding values the company at €325 million.

The investment is a significant vote of confidence in QuTwo's approach to AI and quantum computing. It also highlights the growing interest in European-made companies focused on these areas.
