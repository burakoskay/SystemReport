## Meta AI Launches on WhatsApp

Meta has introduced its AI chatbot, Meta AI, on WhatsApp, promising users private conversations. The new feature, called Incognito Chat, allows users to interact with the AI without anyone else, including Meta, accessing their conversations. This move aligns with Meta's emphasis on user privacy, as personal messages and calls on Meta platforms remain end-to-end encrypted.

The rollout was confirmed by multiple sources, including WIRED and Engadget. According to WIRED, Incognito Chat ensures that "no one other than you will be able to read messages exchanged" with Meta AI.

## How Incognito Chat Works

Incognito Chat on WhatsApp allows users to generate creative text formats, ask questions, and get answers. The AI can help with brainstorming sessions, generate poems, code snippets, scripts, and more. Currently, Meta AI can only read and respond to messages that directly mention "@Meta AI" in a group chat.

## Technical Mechanics

Meta recently released Llama 3, the latest version of its AI-powered large language models. This new model is at least 7 times larger than the previous version and promises to be much more efficient than rival ChatGPT-4. The integration of Llama 3 with WhatsApp's Incognito Chat feature ensures that conversations remain private and secure.

Llama 3's architecture is designed to prioritize efficiency and scalability. It uses a combination of natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to understand and respond to user input. For example, Llama 3 can process and respond to user queries in multiple languages, making it a more versatile and effective chatbot.
