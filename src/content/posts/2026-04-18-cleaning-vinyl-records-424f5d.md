## Introduction to Vinyl Record Cleaning
Clicks and pops on vinyl records aren't supposed to be there. Cleaning gives the music a proper bath.
## Cleaning Methods
The vacuum method removes dust and dirt from the record surface. The brush method is used for gentle cleaning. A cleaning solution can also be applied.
## Choosing the Right Method
For clean records, a brush may be enough. For dirtier records, try a cleaning solution. Always use a safe cleaning solution to preserve sound quality. Clean your records to keep the music sounding its best.
