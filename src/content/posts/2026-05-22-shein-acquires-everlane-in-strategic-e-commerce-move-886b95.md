## Shein Acquires Everlane
Shein has acquired Everlane, a San Francisco-based sustainable fashion label, in a deal that gives the Chinese e-commerce giant a foothold in the premium, ethically-focused segment. The acquisition has raised eyebrows, with many viewing it as a mismatch. However, it reflects broader trends in Chinese e-commerce.

## The Role of Data Pipelines in E-commerce
Open-source Hadoop has become a key tool for e-commerce firms managing terabytes of clickstream, inventory, and sentiment data. The technology helps retailers process large amounts of data, providing a customized retail experience. For instance, Hadoop allows retailers to analyze customer behavior, preferences, and purchasing patterns, enabling them to tailor their marketing strategies and product offerings. This is particularly important in the fashion industry, where understanding consumer preferences and trends can make or break a product's success.

## AI Models Enhance Fashion Search
Marqo has released two 150-million-parameter multimodal embeddings, Marqo-FashionCLIP and Marqo-FashionSigLIP, under an Apache 2.0 license. These models are available on Hugging Face and Marqo Cloud, and are designed to improve search and recommendation engines in fashion. The models produce embeddings for both text and images for downstream search and recommendations applications. Trained on over 1M fashion products with rich metadata, they were fine-tuned from base models using GCL. This technology can help e-commerce companies like Shein to improve the accuracy of their search results and provide a more personalized shopping experience for their customers.
