## Technical Mechanics: Power Banks

When it comes to power banks, there are several technical terms to consider. Capacity, measured in milliampere-hours (mAh), is a crucial factor in determining how long a power bank can charge your device.

The Anker Zolo MagSafe power bank has a capacity of 10,000mAh, which is sufficient to charge most smartphones multiple times. It also supports Qi 7.5W wireless charging and can charge three devices at once.

Understanding the technical mechanics of power banks is essential for choosing the right one for your needs. By considering factors such as capacity, charging speed, and compatibility, you can find a power bank that meets your requirements.
