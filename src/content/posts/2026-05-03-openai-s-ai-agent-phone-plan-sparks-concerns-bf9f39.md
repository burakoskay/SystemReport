## OpenAI's AI Agent Phone Plan Sparks Concerns

OpenAI is reportedly exploring the development of its own phone, with an experience built around AI agents. A recent report suggests that the proposed phone would integrate AI agents into the user interface.

A 9to5Google contributor expressed skepticism about the idea, stating, "I think that sounds horrible." This sentiment highlights concerns about the potential loss of human touch and nuanced communication.
