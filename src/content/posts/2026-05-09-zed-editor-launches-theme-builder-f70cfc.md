## Features and Limitations
The official Zed.dev page details the theme builder's capabilities, including a user-friendly interface for creating and editing themes. The theme builder supports a wide range of customization options, from simple color schemes to complex layouts. Additionally, the theme builder allows users to export and share their themes, which could lead to a community-driven library of user-created themes.

## Technical Mechanics
Under the hood, the theme builder utilizes a combination of JSON and CSS to enable users to create highly customized themes. This approach allows for a high degree of flexibility and control, making it possible for users to tailor their coding environment to their specific needs. The use of JSON and CSS also ensures that the themes are compatible with a wide range of browsers and platforms.
