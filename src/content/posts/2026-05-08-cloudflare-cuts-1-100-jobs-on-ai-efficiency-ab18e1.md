## Cloudflare Cuts 1,100 Jobs on AI Efficiency

Cloudflare announced a large-scale layoff on [date], cutting 1,100 jobs due to efficiency gains from AI. CEO Matthew Prince stated, "Because of AI efficiency gains, we don't need as many support roles."

## Impact on Employees and Operations

The layoff comes as Cloudflare reported a record high revenue. Cloudflare did not provide specific details on the affected positions or the regions where the layoffs occurred. This move has significant implications for the employees affected, who will need to adapt to new opportunities in the rapidly evolving tech landscape. The layoff affects 1,100 employees, a substantial portion of Cloudflare's workforce.
